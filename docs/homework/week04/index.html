Week 04

Week 4: Neural Networks

  • Required:
    • Choose your own adventure homework!
      • (A) Spend an hour working through the Dataquest Deep Learning Fundamentals
        • Add 2-3 sentences to your README about key takeaways related to Neural Networks and Deep Learning.
        • If you get to the project or write some cool code, upload your code to Github!
      • (B) Make a vanilla neural network that can classify MNIST handwritten digits. Data and open source notebooks are available on Kaggle to get you started.
        • MIT also offers a scaffolded notebook here. We highly recommend working through this one!
      • (C) Explore MIT’s free and open-source course on Deep Learning here.
        • Train a neural network to make music by working through this notebook!
  • Suggested/Just for fun:
    • Watch this 3Blue1Brown video explaining how Neural Networks work
    • Read some articles from the reading list under the Week 4 section!
